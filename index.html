ARCHITECT, FOUNDER

Emmanuel Omohodion

Emmanuel Omohodion is a vibrant young architect that exhibit a passionate skill in designs and crafts right from his primary school years, He was however, fortunate to grow up with an understanding parents that saw his passion early and thereabout focusing his academics on his talent by registering him in a technical college for his high school education. Beyond his academic qualifications, Emmanuel continually engages in professional development, staying at the forefront of emerging architectural trends, technologies, and design methodologies.
